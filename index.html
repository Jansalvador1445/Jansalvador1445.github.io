ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Jan Salvador Sebastian, a Mobile Engineer with building apps with a passion for learning and sharing what I've learned. I'm working at mClinica Health Solution Inc. a service that connects pharmacies using mobile technology. This improves patient health by digitizing patient programs, building the capacity of the pharmacy workforce, improving the pharmaceutical supply chain, and generating data from the smallest pharmacy to the biggest chain in Southeast Asia.

2018, I founded Samaritans, Assistances and Reliefs where I am continuously sharing and outreaching people who are in need of help.

2019, I was chosen as one of the core members of Flutter Philippines where we help people to build an application using flutter and dart.

2020, We contribute help for World Health Organization and RapidPass.PH for the COVID-19 application.

2021, I was chosen as one of the moderator of Programmers, Devlopers with a member of 230,000+, to help and to share our knowledge to other developers.
